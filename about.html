PROJECTS

Upon acquiring knowledge in HTML and CSS, I felt compelled to undertake a project, leading me to replicate NETFLIX.COM, a platform dedicated to streaming movies and web series. Through the completion of this project, I discovered numerous new concepts. While designing the initial page, I noticed an issue with color correction and attempted to resolve it using opacity, which proved ineffective. Eventually, I decided to overlay a black div on the image, adjusted its opacity, and successfully achieved the desired effect.
